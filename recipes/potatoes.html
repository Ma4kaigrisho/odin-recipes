Baked Potatoes

Description

Not only that they are healthy but also the baked potatoes and very satiating which is great for people on a diet

Ingrediants

  • 1kg. Potatoes
  • salt
  • universal sauce
  • Black paper
  • water

Steps

  1. Wash and cut the potatoes to small pieces(similar to the photo)
  2. Put some water on the tray and them put the potatoes
  3. put the other Ingrediants and bake for 42 minutes
